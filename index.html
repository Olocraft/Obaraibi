FAQs

What are the eligibility requirements? +
Our basic requirements include being at least 18 years old, having a regular source of income, and a valid bank account. Specific loan products may have additional criteria.
What can I Use as Collateral +
You can use Cars, Landed Properties and other high stake inventory, and a chat with our loan officer will clarify this more for you.
What documents do I need to apply? +
Typically, you'll need ID verification, proof of income, bank statements, and possibly additional documents depending on the loan type.
How long does the approval process take? +
Most applications receive a decision within 24 hours, with funds deposited within 1-3 business days after approval.
What are the interest rates? +
Our interest rates vary based on loan type, term length, your credit profile, and market conditions. Current rates range from 5.99% to 18.99% APR.
