FAQs

What are the eligibility requirements? +
Our basic requirements include being at least 18 years old, having a regular source of income, and a valid bank account. Plus you need to have a valid government issued Identification(NIN,or Passport) and have a Collateral
What can I Use as Collateral +
You can use Cars,Laptops, Household Appliances, Luxury Goods etc. Landed Properties are yet to be accepeted; and a chat with our loan officer will clarify this more for you.
How long does the approval process take? +
As Soon as Collateral is inspected and validated
What documents do I need to apply? +
Typically, you'll need ID verification, proof of income, bank statements, and possibly additional documents depending on the loan type.
What is the minimum amount I can borrow ? +
Our loan amount starts from N50,000.
Can I make mutiple loan applications? +
Yes, you can make as many applications as you want.
What are the interest rates? +
Our interest rates vary based on loan type, term length, your credit profile, and market conditions. Current rates range start from 5.99% to 27.99% depending on the tenure you want.
